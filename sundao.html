ABOUT US

SunDAO is a community controlled DAO, SunDAO's purpose is to develop solutions
that will make the cryptocurrency space safer and more equal for everyone.

The deflationary mechanism of the SunDAO token ($SDAO) includes a redistribution
system. 3% of each transaction is distributed as a reward to holders.

On top of this, 20% of the total $SDAO supply will be burned, liquidity will be locked
for 1 YEAR with the use of Anti-Whale 2.0 Encrypted Contracts.
